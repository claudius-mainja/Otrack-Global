COMPLETE ECOSYSTEM

More Than Just Tracking

Our comprehensive tools and features designed to optimize every aspect of your fleet operations

Driver Behavior Analysis

Monitor and improve driver performance with detailed analytics on harsh braking, acceleration, cornering, and speeding. Promote safer driving habits and reduce accident risks through data-driven insights and automated coaching.

Customizable Reporting

Generate comprehensive reports tailored to your business needs. Track mileage, fuel consumption, idle time, maintenance schedules, and more. Export data in multiple formats for seamless integration with your existing systems and workflows.

International Roaming

Track your vehicles across borders with our reliable international roaming capabilities. Maintain full visibility and control of your fleet whether operating locally or across multiple countries in Southern Africa and beyond.

Maintenance Management

Stay ahead of vehicle maintenance with automated reminders based on mileage, engine hours, or time intervals. Reduce downtime, extend vehicle lifespan, and maintain optimal fleet performance with proactive maintenance scheduling.

Smart Alerts & Notifications

Receive instant notifications via SMS, email, or mobile app for critical events including speeding, geofence violations, unauthorized usage, and emergency situations. Stay informed and respond quickly to any fleet incidents.

Mobile App Access

Manage your fleet on-the-go with our intuitive mobile applications for iOS and Android. Access real-time tracking, receive alerts, generate reports, and control your entire fleet from the palm of your hand, anytime, anywhere.
